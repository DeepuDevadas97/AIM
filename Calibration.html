CALIBRATION

WHAT DO WE CALIBRATE?
OVERVIEW

AIM provides instruments calibration service to various industries across Saudi Arabia. Our highly qualified and experienced technicians will perform this scope of work daily and we are ready to deliver this high quality service according to schedule.We give clients the confidence that their equipment is taking accurate measurements and can stand up to any inspectional or auditing process.

We will provide Calibration Certificates and other required documentation with each instrument after performing the calibration either on-site at your facility or in our calibration laboratory.

OFF SITE CALIBRATION SERVICES

Our complete metrology lab is fully equipped with state of the art precision instrumentation providing a broad range of calibrations for Dimensional, Electronic, and Process equipment. We are constantly adding new instruments and ideas in order to be the leader in precision calibration. All calibrations are performed by trained personnel in compliances with industry recognized standards. It is an fast turnaround time, high quality services at a reasonable rate, wide range of services and clients. While almost all of our offerings can be done in your facility, our specialized lab allows us to offer extended services including the calibration of gauge blocks, plain ring gages, radius gagr sets, and torque..etc. We guarantee your equipment meets industry standards, save on the cost of upkeep, and ensure customers feel confident in your machines with an credibile certification of calibration.

ON-SITE CALIBRATION SERVICES

On site calibration is a multiple occasions when a calibration must be performed at the customer’s premises and not a calibration laboratory.There is equipment that is to large and fixed and cannot be moved.scales,cranes,large containers,big ovens and refrigerators are some examples of such equipment that is impossible to be removed and sent for calibration.This kind of equipment must be calibrated on site,and that’s what AIM Calibration Services are here . There are also other instruments which are part of an installation and they must be calibrated as a whole where they must be part of an industrial process such as temperature and pressure transmitters,pressure gauges and flow transmitters which are observed and controllers or PLC.There are also instruments that are very sensitive and their transportation to and from the calibration laboratory may cause damage.
